## â“ FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between Study Mode and Quiz Mode?**
A: Study Mode shows flashcards you flip over. Quiz Mode tests you with multiple-choice questions.

**Q: Why are there 4 options?**
A: Multiple-choice format is research-proven to enhance learning through active recall.

**Q: Can I see the example sentence in Quiz Mode?**
A: Yes! If the card has an example, it appears below the options to help you learn in context.

**Q: Does my quiz score get saved?**
A: No, quiz attempts are not saved (by design). Focus on learning, not scores!

**Q: What if I skip a question?**
A: Skipped questions don't count toward your score, but they're still included in the total.

**Q: Can I go back and review my answers?**
A: Not in the current quiz, but you can take the quiz again after returning to Study Mode.

**Q: How many questions are in a quiz?**
A: As many as there are cards in your deck. Each card becomes one question.

**Q: What if I have fewer than 4 cards?**
A: Quiz Mode needs at least 4 cards (for 4 options). Add more cards to your deck first!

## ğŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### Problem: "No options showing"
**Solution**: Make sure your deck has at least 4 cards. Quiz Mode needs multiple cards to generate options.

### Problem: "Quiz seems too easy/hard"
**Solution**: This is normal! Try focusing on the cards you get wrong and review them in Study Mode.

### Problem: "Can't click options"
**Solution**: Wait for the question to fully load. Sometimes there's a brief delay after selecting an answer.

### Problem: "Score not incrementing correctly"
**Solution**: Skipped questions don't count. Only answered questions add to your score.
